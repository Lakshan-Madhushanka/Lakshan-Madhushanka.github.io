About

Biography

I’m Lakshan Madhushanka, an Electrical and Electronic Engineer turned Lecturer in Computer Engineering with a deep passion for Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Intelligent Systems. I graduated from the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya, where I specialized in Communication, Power Systems, and Advanced Control Systems.

Currently, I serve as a Lecturer at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, where I contribute to research at the AI and Biomedical Engineering Research Group, focusing on intelligent healthcare systems and medical imaging.

My work has been nationally recognized with awards including the Silver Medal from the Sri Lanka Inventors Commission, the Eng. E.W. Karunarathna Award from the Institution of Engineers, Sri Lanka, the W.P. Jayasekara Prize for the best undergraduate project in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, and the Prof. Suhada Jayasuriya Scholarship Grant for the best Robotics and Control project.

Beyond academia, I am an active IEEE volunteer at section, regional, and global levels, having served with IEEE HTB, IEEE Region 10 SAC, and the IEEE Sri Lanka Section. I was honored as an IEEE STEM Champion in 2023 and received the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Outstanding Volunteer Award (Student Category) in 2022.

Awards & Recognition

Sep 2023

IEEE R10 Ethics Champion 2023

Presented by: IEEE R10 Ethics Committee

Lakshan was recognized as IEEE R10 Ethics Champion 2023 for his commitment to ethical standards in technology. His dedication contributed to elevating IEEE Sri Lanka Section to a top 5 section in the region.

May 2023

[Winner] IEEE STEM Champion 2023

Presented by: IEEE Education Activities

The IEEE STEM Champion title is a designation given to an individual IEEE volunteer. It was developed to recognize IEEE volunteers for their contributions to pre-university STEM education and to build a community of pre-university IEEE STEM outreach volunteers. In 2023, a total of 26 champions were selected by the IEEE Education Activities Committee, covering all regions. Lakshan is the second person from IEEE Sri Lanka Section to receive the honor.

Feb 2023

[Graduated] IEEE MGA VoLT Program 2022

Presented by: IEEE MGA

IEEE VoLT program is organized by IEEE MGA for volunteers who have the potential to be future leaders of IEEE. Lakshan was selected for this program representing the Sri Lanka Section. Only 65 members (graduate students or above) from around the world are selected for this program each year. Lakshan graduated from the program successfully.

Feb 2023

[Winner] Best Affinity Group Project Award 2022 (Section Affinity Group Category)

Presented by: IEEE Sri Lanka Section

Lakshan initiated and led the 'Sihinayata Peraman' project as the Chair of the IEEE Sri Lanka Section SIGHT Group. This career guidance and community service project was conducted on August 14th at Thudugala, Sri Buddharakhithrama Watadageya Maha Viharaya. Under Lakshan's leadership, the project benefited over 100 students in grades 8, 9, and 10, providing them with valuable career guidance. This project was awarded as the best affinity group project at the section awards night. The purpose of the award associated with this project is to improve the quality of projects organized by IEEE Section Affinity Groups in Sri Lanka and to encourage more creative ideas with significant impact.

This award aims to recognize the work done by an individual student member from the IEEE Sri Lanka Section who has made exemplary contributions at the Sectional, Regional, and Global levels.
