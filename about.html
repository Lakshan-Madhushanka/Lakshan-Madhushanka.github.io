About

I’m an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate (University of Peradeniya) and currently a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka. My interests include biomedical image processing, embedded systems, and machine learning. I’ve worked with OCT scanners, slide scanners, Jetson devices, and ROS-based robots.

Research Focus

  • OCT-based plant & oral cancer imaging
  • Histopathology grading with CNNs & Transformers
