About

I’m an Electrical and Electronic Engineering Graduate of the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. Currently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka. My interests include biomedical image processing, embedded systems, and machine learning. I’ve worked with OCT scanners, slide scanners, Jetson devices, and ROS-based robots.
